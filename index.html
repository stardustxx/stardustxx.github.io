Hey

Remind people of what they need to do

Hey focuses on setting up tasks between people. You can send a task to another user right now or at the designated time. This was my capstone project on Udacity Android Nanodegree and I will continue maintaining it to solve the problem where people forget what they need to do after they were told.

Technology: Android, Firebase, Content Provider, Loader, Material Design, Intent Service, Alarm Manager, Google Services
